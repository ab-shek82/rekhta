CATEGORY : AASHIQ

Noun, masculine, root: Arabic

EXPLAINATION OF SAUDAA

In Urdu poetry, the word saudaa is often used to show the lover’s sadness or madness at the separation from his beloved. It also refers to his burning desire to find love, meet the beloved or solace.
