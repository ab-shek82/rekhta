Ishq bahrūp thā jo chashm-o-dil-o-sar meñ rahā

kahīñ hairat kahīñ vahshat kahīñ saudā ban kar

~ Jaleel Manakpuri

translation

With this obsession of knowledge, these journals and books, is desired, but, the erasure of memories of a person

Read More

Explanation

The poet asserts that his passion for knowledge and indulgence in books are aimed only at forgetting a person. Since it is not possible to get rid of passion, he diverts it from his beloved to knowledge in the hope of forgetting her.

शाइ’र कहता है की इ’ल्म और ककताबों के ललए उसकी दीवानगी ककसी शख़्स को भूलने की कोलशश भर है चूूँकक शाइ’र का ददमाग़ लगातार उसे महबूब की याद ददलाता है, इसललए वो इ’ल्म की तरफ़ बढ़ने लगता है ताकक ददमाग़ कहीीं और उलझा रहे।
